How SkillConnect Makes Money

Transparent, fair, and designed to let students and startups both win.

Platform Commission
We charge a small commission on each completed project transaction. Students keep the majority of earnings while we handle payments, escrow, and dispute resolution.
8–12%
of project value (paid by startup)
